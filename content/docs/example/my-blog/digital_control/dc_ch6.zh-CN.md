# 6 Stability

## 6.1 Asymptotic stability

> A system is asymptotically stable if its steady-state (i.e. at infinite time) response to any initial condition decays to zero.

如果一个系统对于任何初始输入条件最后都可以收敛到0, 那么我们可以称之为**渐进稳定**. 用数学表达即是:
\[y(\infty)=0, \forall y(0)\]
